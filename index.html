HANDS UP!

Join us on September 21 in HKUST’s first-ever attempt to break a Guinness World Record.

We will attempt to break the most handprints on a wall painting in an hour. This will also help raise funds for the dream chaser scholarship. This is open to ALL HKUST students, staff, and alumni. Joining is completely FREE! Register here as a participant or click here to donate.

Dream Chaser Scholarship Fund

The Scholarship

The Dream Chaser Scholarship Fund aims to help students who are in need of financial aid. It is a need-based and merit-based scholarship built on the shared belief that passionate and competent students should be given the means to wholeheartedly pursue their dreams.
